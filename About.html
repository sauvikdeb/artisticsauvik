About Me

I'm an Artist, Illustrator & Graphic Designer born and raised in Assam, India.

I'm currently pursuing my B.Com while working on multiple freelance and personal projects ranging from illustrations, portraits, character designs and many other forms.

Besides doing digital artworks, I've also worked extensively with traditional media. I try to achieve a sense of depth with my digital works using the same techniques I would use if I were working in traditional.

Exploring different creative possibilities, while also having fun, is what I love to do.
